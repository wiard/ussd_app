Touting — your way through Africa

A simple USSD system that makes existing economic activity visible — by choice, not by force. Drivers choose when they’re available. Customers choose once. The network moves.

What it is

USSD is the interface: a flat menu that works on every phone.

Drivers & businesses become reachable without apps, data bundles, or downloads.

SACCOs are the trust anchor: membership, standards, mediation, community legitimacy.

Touts (Gen Z) drive adoption on the ground: onboarding + activation + accuracy.

Why Transport is the smartest start

Transport is the hardest test: availability, speed, and trust. If we can make transport reliable, we unlock value for customers, businesses, and SACCOs automatically.

No guessing. No wasted calls. No algorithm.

The core flow

1) Driver sets AVAILABLE (voluntary)
2) Customer opens USSD menu and selects a driver/business
3) Session closes
4) Action happens (call/SMS/callback)
5) Driver becomes UNAVAILABLE after connection (keeps list honest)

Why this is different from Uber/Bolt

Uber/Bolt: algorithms decide visibility, rules come from above, commissions extract value.

This system: visibility is chosen by people, trust comes from community, the menu reveals reality.

Why Africa’s Talking is a natural partner

We keep the product lean: USSD reveals reality; SMS/voice turns it into action. That creates real USSD + messaging volume and supports mass adoption with low friction.

Mission: People stay in control. The system simply listens.

Economic activity becomes visible by choice — using tools Africa already trusts.
